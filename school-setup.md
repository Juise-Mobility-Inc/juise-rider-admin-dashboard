# Set up your school

Complete school configuration before opening parking or mobility programs to students.

## Recommended setup order

1. Complete **School Profile**.
2. Add **School Terms**.
3. Configure **Registration Fees Setup** and **Violation Fees**.
4. Add **School Zones** and **School POIs**.
5. Create **Juise Packs** and their parking spots.
6. Review vehicle registrations and pending reservations.
7. Add challenges and notifications when needed.

## School Profile

Use **School Profile** to manage the school name, campus defaults, logo, colors, and active status. Save the profile before uploading media or configuring other campus features.

## School Terms

A term is the date range used by term-based programs such as parking reservations.

1. Open **School Terms**.
2. Add a term and enter a recognizable name.
3. Set its start and end dates.
4. Confirm that the dates do not unintentionally overlap another term.
5. Save the displayed term list.

Changing or removing a term can affect how existing reservations are displayed. Review current reservations before changing published dates.

## School Zones

Use **School Zones** to define geographic riding rules:

- A no-go zone identifies an area where riding is not permitted.
- A speed-limit zone identifies an area with a maximum riding speed.

Draw or import the boundary, select the appropriate zone type, and review enforcement tiers carefully. An enforcement tier may issue a warning, deduct points, notify a student, or require administrative review. Zone changes generally apply to future activity rather than recalculating past rides.

## School POIs

A point of interest (POI) is a mapped destination or check-in area. Enter its coordinates, check-in radius, bonus points, and active status. Confirm the physical location on the map before saving.

When importing zones or POIs from CSV, inspect the preview and verify coordinates, distance units, and reward values before completing the import.
