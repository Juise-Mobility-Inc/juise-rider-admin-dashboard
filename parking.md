# Manage parking

## Create and maintain Juise Packs

A **Juise Pack** is a school-managed parking location. A pack contains one or more individual spots.

1. Open **Juise Packs**.
2. Create a pack and enter its name, description, campus, and location.
3. Add the required number of spots.
4. Save and confirm that the pack appears in the list and on the map.
5. Generate or download pack and spot QR codes when needed.

Match every printed QR code to the correct physical location. A misplaced spot code can produce incorrect check-ins or reservations.

## Review pending reservations

Open **Pending Reservations** to process student requests.

Before deciding, confirm the student, school term, pack, spot, and registered device. Select **Approve** to accept the request or **Deny** to reject it. Both actions remove the request from the pending queue and may immediately change what the student can access.

## Review parking incidents

Use **Parking Reports** to review reported parking incidents. Open a report to see its student, device, location, evidence, status, and history when available.

When changing a report:

1. Review all submitted details and media.
2. Confirm that you are viewing the correct incident.
3. Select the appropriate status or resolution.
4. Add clear administrative context when a note field is available.
5. Save and verify the updated status.

## View parking locations

Use **Map Overview** for a combined view of mapped school resources. Use **Sightings Map** when you need to review reported device or beacon locations. Map markers are operational aids and may reflect delayed or incomplete reports.
