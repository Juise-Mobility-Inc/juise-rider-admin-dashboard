# Manage students and vehicles

## Find a student

Open **Students** to view the school roster. Search or filter the list, then select a student to open their detailed record.

A student record may include membership information, registered devices, reservations, ride history, POI visits, violations, challenge activity, and media. Use **Copy ID** when support asks for a student or record identifier.

## Review student activity

Use the student detail view to understand a record in context. Check dates and statuses before acting: an old ride, expired term, or resolved violation may not require new action.

The **Student Leaderboard** compares eligible student activity. Treat rankings as an engagement view rather than an official academic or disciplinary record.

## Review vehicle registrations

Open **Vehicle Registrations** to review submitted registrations.

Before approving or declining a registration:

1. Confirm the student identity.
2. Compare the submitted device or vehicle information with any available images.
3. Check the current review status and history.
4. Confirm the applicable registration fee rule.
5. Approve or decline only after the record is complete.

Approval makes the registration eligible for the supported campus workflow. Declining rejects the submission and may require the student to correct and resubmit it.

## Manage registration fees

Use **Registration Fees Setup** to define fees associated with device or vehicle registration. Confirm the amount, effective status, and intended audience before activating a rule. Avoid creating overlapping active rules unless your organization has a documented reason.

## Monitor campus devices

Use **Campus Devices** to review registered device inventory and recent beacon information. Location or sighting data reflects the most recently reported information and may not represent a device’s live position.
