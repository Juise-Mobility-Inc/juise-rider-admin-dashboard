# Manage safety and violations

## Review ride penalties

Open **Penalty Reports** or **Ride Information** to inspect detected riding events. A report may include the student, ride session, zone, time, coordinates, speed, penalty rule, and review status.

Before resolving a report, compare the event with the applicable zone and enforcement policy. Map and sensor data can provide useful context but should be reviewed alongside the complete record.

## Review parking violations

Open a parking violation to review its current status, history, associated student or device, and supporting media. If the dashboard permits an update, choose the status that reflects your organization’s completed review and save the change.

Do not create duplicate actions for the same incident. Check the history first to see whether another administrator has already reviewed it.

## Configure violation fees

Use **Violation Fees** to create or update fee rules associated with parking violations.

Before activating a rule, verify:

- the violation category;
- the fee amount;
- the rule’s active status;
- the school policy authorizing the fee; and
- whether another active rule already covers the same event.

Fee-rule changes may affect new violations without changing historical charges. Use your organization’s financial process for reconciliation and refunds.

## Preserve evidence

Download reports or record relevant IDs before making a significant status change. Do not upload sensitive or unrelated material as violation evidence. Follow your school’s retention and privacy policies when handling student information.
